Shelter Updates:

During the COVID-19 Pandemic, our hours have been shortened. Please call if you'd like to come later in the day. If interested in adopting, we ask that the latest you arrive is a half hour before closing.

Adoption Process

TLC does not use application forms, so there is nothing you need to fill out prior to visiting the Shelter. The Shelter staff will talk to you about adopting an animal and how you will care for it. You will be approved or denied based on your response (we only want what is best for the animal and you and your family).

When you come in looking for a pet, you must bring your children. If interested in adopting a puppy or dog please bring any other dogs so we can see how they interact.

If you rent an apartment or own a condominium, you must bring a copy of your lease or your condominium bylaws stating that you are able to have pets on the premises. We need proof of where you live : drivers license, utility bill etc.

Adoption Fees

Dog and puppy adoptions fees will start at $300.00 along with whatever the cost the shelter has incurred having the animals spayed/neutered prior to adoption. All animals will be microchipped at time of adoption. Vaccinations will be included appropriate for the age of the dog/puppy along with wormings.
